# St. Joseph Medical Center in Tacoma

1717 South J Street

Tacoma, WA 98405

#### [253-426-4101](tel:2534264101)

Serving Tacoma and surrounding areas, St. Joseph Medical Center is consistently ranked among the top hospitals in Washington by U.S. News & World Report. Our compassionate team of experts are ready to help you and your family achieve your best health, throughout all the stages of your life. Find a Virginia Mason Franciscan Health [primary or specialty provider](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/about-vmfh/our-medical-groups.html "find a primary or specialty provider") at clinics close to where you live or work.

## Our health care services

We’ve built our programs based on your needs, recruiting the top specialists and investing in advanced technology. On-site services include:

- A [Family Birth Center](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/our-services/pregnancy-childbirth/labor-and-delivery/birth-centers/st-joseph-tacoma.html "Family Birth Center") with a Level III neonatal intensive care unit (NICU) and our new [Midwifery Center at St. Joseph](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/our-services/pregnancy-childbirth/labor-and-delivery/birth-centers/midwifery-birth-center.html "Midwifery Center at St. Joseph")
- Convenient [diagnostic imaging and radiology scheduling services](https://www.vmfh.org/our-services/imaging-radiology/radiology-services.html "Diagnostic imaging and radiology services")
- 24-hour, around-the-clock [emergency and trauma care services](https://www.vmfh.org/our-hospitals/st-joseph-medical-center/emergency-care.html "emergency and trauma care services"), including the premier Level II Trauma Center serving Tacoma and the South Sound
- [Cancer care](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/our-hospitals/st-joseph-medical-center/cancer-care.html "Cancer care") from the Puget Sound’s leading cancer specialists
- [Heart care](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/our-services/cardiovascular-health.html "Heart care") at the South Sound’s largest heart and vascular center
- [Orthopedics care](https://www.vmfh.org/our-services/orthopedics-and-sports-medicine.html "Orthopedics care") **,** including a [joint replacement program](https://www.vmfh.org/our-services/orthopedics-and-sports-medicine/joint-replacement.html "Joint replacement program") that features a dedicated nurse navigator and care coordinator
- [Neuroscience services](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/our-services/neurosciences-spine.html "Neuroscience services"), including leading-edge [Gamma Knife](https://www.vmfh.org/our-services/center-for-neurosciences-spine/neurosurgery.html "Gamma Knife") radiosurgery for brain tumors
- A Level II Trauma Center with board-certified trauma surgeons and doctors and registered nurses certified in emergency medicine and advanced cardiac life support. Learn more about our [Trauma Services](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/our-services/trauma-services.html "learn about trauma services").
- Requesting [medical records](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/patient-and-visitor-information/patient-information/requesting-medical-records.html "requesting medical records")
- And many [more services](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/our-hospitals/st-joseph-medical-center/patient-visitors.html "More services")

Learn more about [diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging](https://www.vmfh.org/content/vmfhorg/en/about-vmfh/deib.html "diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging") at St. Joseph Medical Center.
