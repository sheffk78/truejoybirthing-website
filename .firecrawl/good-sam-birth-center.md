## Postpartum exercise and education program

In partnership with [Olympic Sports & Spine](https://www.osstherapy.com/), we now offer a six-week program designed to help postpartum individuals restore strength, function and confidence through guided pelvic floor exercises and education.

[Register for Our Postpartum Program](https://www.multicare.org/classes-events/?query=&tag=25)

From our spacious birthing suites, to the support and care we provide during and after your labor and delivery, the Family Birth Center at MultiCare Good Samaritan Hospital is a warm and welcoming place for you to have your baby. Our birth center features include:

- Private birthing suites, where you’ll stay throughout your labor, delivery and recovery (unless mom needs extra medical support)
- Each birthing suite includes sleeping couches, rockers, jetted tubs, refrigerators, and plenty of room for visitors
- Wireless monitoring for laboring mobility
- All our postpartum rooms are also private, with windows, sleeping couches, rockers, refrigerators and room for visitors
- Baby stays in the room with mom (unless baby needs extra medical support)
- Level II neonatal intensive care unit (NICU) onsite with 24/7 coverage from board-certified neonatology specialists, if your baby needs more care
- A family-friendly, open visitor policy so friends, family and loved ones can visit whenever you like
- [Lactation support](https://www.multicare.org/services/obstetrics-gynecology/obstetrics/postpartum-newborn-care/breastfeeding-lactation-support/) to help you successfully breastfeed your baby
- Support for vaginal birth after cesarean (VBAC)
- Wireless monitoring for laboring mobility
- Birthing peanuts, cub cushions, slings and an array of support for unmedicated laboring
- Nitrous oxide for pain management
- Gentle C-sections available by request, including better visibility of baby’s birth and faster skin-to-skin contact
- [Cord blood banking](https://www.multicare.org/services/obstetrics-gynecology/obstetrics/postpartum-newborn-care/cord-blood-banking/)

## Enhancing communication, improving outcomes

The Birth Center participates in TeamBirth, an industry-standard process to improve communication, teamwork and shared decision making throughout your birthing process for a patient-centered experience.
