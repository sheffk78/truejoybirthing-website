5. Birth Center

The Family Birth Center at MultiCare Tacoma General Hospital offers a welcoming environment, the latest amenities and the most up-to-date medical technology to help make your birth experience a positive one.

When you choose to have your baby at our Birth Center you have access to:

- All-private birthing suites, where you’ll stay throughout your labor, delivery and recovery (Unless you need extra medical support).
- Each room includes a window, en suite bathroom and jetted tub, plenty of room for visitors and a designated family zone with Internet access, phone charging, storage area and large sleeper sofas to accommodate overnight visitors.
- Private, spacious postpartum rooms where mom, baby and family can rest until it’s time to leave the hospital.
- Baby stays in the room with mom (unless baby needs extra medical support).
- [Lactation support](https://www.multicare.org/services/obstetrics-gynecology/obstetrics/postpartum-newborn-care/breastfeeding-lactation-support/) to help you successfully breast feed your baby.
- On-site lactation supply boutique that provides breast pump rental and sales, as well as other supplies for sale including nursing bras and bra fittings.

In case your baby needs a higher level of care, the [neonatal intensive care unit (NICU) at Mary Bridge Children’s Hospital](https://www.marybridge.org/childrens-hospital/hospital-care/neonatal-intensive-care-unit-nicu/) is at the ready. As the region’s only Level IV NICU, we are able to care for the smallest, sickest and most fragile newborns.

## Enhancing communication, improving outcomes

The Birth Center participates in TeamBirth, an industry-standard process to improve communication, teamwork and shared decision making throughout your birthing process for a patient-centered experience.
