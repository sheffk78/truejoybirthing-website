Questions

Common Questions for New Doulas

Do I need to be certified to use these tools?

No. The templates and education are useful whether you're certified, in training, or still exploring birth work. Many of our users are volunteer doulas or midwifery students.

How is this different from other doula business courses?

Most courses teach you how to market yourself. We teach you how to actually serve clients well — with tools that make you look organized, informed, and trustworthy from day one.

Can I share these templates with my clients?

Yes. The birth plan builder in the free app is designed for exactly this. Your clients build their plan, and it syncs to your Pro Mode dashboard. No extra license needed.

What if I'm not tech-savvy?

The app is designed to be simple. If you can use Instagram, you can use this. And Pro Mode walks you through everything step by step.
