Why I built this

I'm Shelbi Kohler — certified birth doula and childbirth educator. I've supported hundreds of families through births in hospitals, birth centers, and at home.

I kept seeing the same thing: moms who wanted to feel prepared but didn't know their options, didn't have the words, and felt like the system was happening to them instead of with them.

So I built something practical. A birth plan that actually works in a hospital room. Education that explains the tradeoffs, not just the buzzwords. And for doulas — tools that finally make running a practice feel organized instead of overwhelming.

Hear why Shelbi became a doula and what she's building.

What makes this different

  • Doula-crafted, not generic. Every template, every walkthrough, every script is built from real birth-room experience.
  • Born connected. When a mom builds her plan in the free app, it flows directly into her doula's Pro dashboard. No lost details. No copy-paste.
  • Honest, not fear-based. We explain options and tradeoffs. We don't scare you into decisions.
