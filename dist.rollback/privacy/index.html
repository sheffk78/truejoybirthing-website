Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

True Joy Birthing, LLC ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account information:

Name, email address, and password when you create an account.

Birth plan data:

Preferences, choices, and notes you enter into the birth plan builder.

Pro Mode data:

Client information, visit notes, contracts, and invoices you create as a doula or midwife.

Usage data:

How you interact with the app, including pages visited, features used, and session duration.

Device information:

Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting and optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process subscriptions, send transactional communications (account confirmation, subscription receipts, renewal notices), respond to support requests, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Subscription Information

If you subscribe to True Joy Pro, we collect and process your subscription status, payment method details (processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play), and transaction history. We do not store your full payment card number on our servers. Subscription payments and refunds are managed by Apple or Google through your device's account settings.

True Joy Pro pricing: $29.99/month or $274.99/year (annual saves $85.89). New subscribers receive a 14-day free trial. Subscriptions auto-renew. Cancel anytime in your device's subscription settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • ● Apple / Google: Payment processing, subscription management, and app delivery.
  • ● Stripe: Payment processing for Pro subscriptions and consults (if applicable).
  • ● Analytics tools (GA4): Aggregated, anonymized usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is shared.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for analytics.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us. If you are a California resident, you have the right to request a copy of your data under the CCPA. To exercise these rights, email support@truejoybirthing.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

True Joy Birthing is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Medical Disclaimer

True Joy Birthing provides educational content and planning tools. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions related to your pregnancy, birth, or postpartum care.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy with a revised "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@truejoybirthing.com.
